The PNX Promise

The PNX Promise represents our unwavering Client Guarantee, delivering complete satisfaction at every step of the imaging journey. Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc. proudly honors all warranties and includes an additional year of basic maintenance coverage with every x-ray system purchased or leased through us, ensuring continued confidence long after installation. Our 24/7 Client Hotline provides direct access to knowledgeable support whenever and wherever it’s needed, minimizing downtime and maximizing performance. With transparency, responsiveness, and reliability at the core of our service approach, you can trust that PNX is with you every step of the way.
