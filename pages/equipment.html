SR-130 X-Ray Generator System

The SR-130 provides higher power, combined with state of the art technology, system reliability and low maintenance, all in a small footprint, lightweight package. The SR-130 provides hospital quality imaging performed anywhere, anytime a portable X-Ray System is transported to a patient.

PowerMax 1260 X-Ray Generator System

The PowerMax 1260 provides the maximum power obtainable from a line powered portable with state of the art technology, system reliability and low maintenance, all in a small footprint, lightweight package. The PowerMax 1260 is a 120kV, 15-60mA system. With 3kW across the full range, it can reduce exposure times on difficult radiographic anatomical views, thereby minimizing patient motion problems and enhancing image quality.

JPI’s DirectVet Digital X-Ray System

JPI’s DirectVet 110-volt Veterinary Digital X-Ray System is an ergonomically designed digital X-Ray System that runs on a standard wall outlet and is powerful enough to replace a system that requires high voltage. The DirectVet System combines our robust ExamVue Software, easy-to-install Vet-view Veterinary Table, and quality, cost-effective digital detector for a complete system that can be set up in one hour. The DirectVet system gives you quality digital x-rays, allowing your practice to hit the ground running at an attractive cost without special preparations, power supplies, or construction.

JPI's Podiatric X-Ray System

The Advance Step is a dedicated podiatry X-ray system which provides superior imaging performance for podiatry radiology. Fully weight balanced, and capable of 90 degrees of angular movement, with 8" of linear front to back movement. The Advance Step orthoposer is easy to position for any foot or ankle shot!

JPI's 240HF Non-Human Use X-Ray System

Portable Veterinary Mixed Animal X-Ray Generator is a High frequency inverter type generator. Digital displays of kV, mAs and exposure time (sec.). It has Self-manageable APR function with memory key.
