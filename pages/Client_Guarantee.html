PNX Client Guarantee

Our commitment goes beyond delivering exceptional products—it’s about standing beside our clients at every stage of their success. We believe trust is earned through consistency, accountability, and proactive support, not promises alone. From first conversation to long after installation, we operate as a partner invested in your outcomes. Your confidence in working with us is the standard by which we measure ourselves.

We believe every client deserves to feel informed, respected, and supported... never rushed, overlooked, or uncertain. When you work with us, you can expect the following:

  • Transparent Return Policies
    Clear, fair return options communicated upfront, with no hidden conditions or surprises.
  • Honored Guarantees & Warranties
    All manufacturer warranties fully upheld, plus our commitment to stand behind every solution we deliver.
  • Reliable Service & Support
    Responsive service, knowledgeable technicians, and dependable follow-through—before, during, and after delivery.
  • Clear, Proactive Communication
    Realistic timelines, timely updates, and prompt responses, so you always know what to expect.
  • Advance Notifications
    Early notice of maintenance needs, service schedules, upgrades, or changes that may affect your equipment or operations.
  • Respect for Your Time & Business
    Appointments honored, preparation completed in advance, and solutions designed around your workflow—not ours.
  • A Voice That’s Heard
    Your feedback matters. We listen, adapt, and continuously improve based on your experience.
  • A True Partnership
    You’re not just a customer—you’re a valued partner, supported with care, professionalism, and long-term commitment.

We’re Here When You Need Us

If you have questions, concerns, or need clarification about your equipment or order, or a scheduled service, our team is ready to help. Contact Pacific Northwest X-Ray at 1-800-438-1000 for clear answers, practical solutions, and responsive support you can rely on today.
