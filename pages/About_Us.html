About Us

Pacific Northwest X-Ray was founded when a veterinarian, a physician, and an x-ray technologist recognized a shared challenge: imaging systems often lacked the clarity, reliability, and responsive support that care teams require. Each brought their expertise, animal health, clinical medicine and technical precision to the table united by a vision of better service and lasting trust.

Together, they built Pacific Northwest X-Ray not just as an equipment provider, but as partner committed to excellence. From portable units to advanced platforms, every system reflects their promise: empowering professionals with technology that elevates care, strengthens workflows, and endures through every patient, every day.
